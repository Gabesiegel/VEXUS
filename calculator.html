About VExUS

The Venous Excess UltraSound (VExUS) grading system is a point-of-care ultrasound technique for assessing systemic venous congestion. It combines measurements of inferior vena cava diameter with Doppler patterns of hepatic, portal, and intrarenal veins to provide a comprehensive evaluation of venous congestion.
