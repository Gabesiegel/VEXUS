About VExUS ATLAS

VExUS ATLAS represents a breakthrough in medical imaging analysis, combining advanced artificial intelligence with clinical expertise in venous congestion assessment. Our mission is to enhance patient care through innovative technology and evidence-based medicine.

Our Vision

VExUS ATLAS aims to revolutionize the way healthcare professionals assess venous congestion by providing cutting-edge AI-powered tools that improve diagnostic accuracy and efficiency. We strive to make advanced venous ultrasound assessment accessible to clinicians worldwide.

Our platform integrates state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms with clinical expertise to offer reliable, evidence-based analysis of ultrasound images, helping clinicians make more informed decisions for patient care.

Our Approach

The VExUS (Venous Excess Ultrasound Score) method evaluates venous congestion by examining the inferior vena cava, hepatic veins, portal veins, and renal veins. This comprehensive approach provides a more complete picture of a patient's venous congestion status.

Our AI algorithms are trained on extensive datasets of validated ultrasound images, ensuring high accuracy in classifying different venous waveform patterns. This technology helps standardize the assessment process and reduces interobserver variability.
